# Scenario 3: Borderline

Taxpayer: Alice Carter, age 45.

Candidate dependent: Emma Carter, age 23.

Facts:

- Emma is Alice's niece.
- Emma is a full-time student.
- Emma lived with Alice for 7 months of the tax year.
- Emma is not disabled.
- Emma provided 49% of her own support.
- Alice provided 51% of Emma's support.
- Emma did not file a joint return.
- Emma is a U.S. citizen.
- There is no competing claimant.

Why this is borderline:

- Emma is very close to the age cutoff.
- She barely satisfies the more-than-half-year residence rule.
- She is very close to providing over half of her own support.

Expected result:

- Emma is a dependent of Alice as a qualifying child, but only narrowly.
