# Scenario 1: Dependent

Taxpayer: Alice Carter, age 45.

Candidate dependent: Ben Carter, age 17.

Facts:

- Ben is Alice's biological son.
- Ben lived with Alice for all 12 months of the tax year.
- Ben is a U.S. citizen.
- Ben did not file a joint return with a spouse.
- Ben provided only 10% of his own support.
- Alice provided 80% of Ben's support.
- There is no competing claimant.

Expected result:

- Ben is a dependent of Alice.
- More specifically, Ben qualifies as a qualifying child.
