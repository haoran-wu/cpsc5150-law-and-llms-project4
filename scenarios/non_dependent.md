# Scenario 2: Non-Dependent

Taxpayer: Alice Carter, age 45.

Candidate dependent: Carla Carter, age 26.

Facts:

- Carla is Alice's sister.
- Carla lived in another city and did not share Alice's principal place of abode for more than half the year.
- Carla earned $12,000 of gross income during the year.
- Alice provided only 30% of Carla's support.
- Carla did not file a joint return.
- Carla is a U.S. citizen.
- There is no competing claimant.

Expected result:

- Carla is not a dependent of Alice.
- She is not a qualifying child because she is too old and did not live with Alice for more than half the year.
- She is not a qualifying relative because her income is above the exemption threshold used in the model and Alice did not provide over half of her support.
