# Part B LLM Quick Answer Record

## Prompt

Consider this tax scenario and answer briefly whether Noah is Maya's dependent under 26 U.S.C. §152.

- Taxpayer: Maya Lopez, age 41
- Other person: Noah Lopez, age 18
- Noah is Maya's son
- Noah lived with Maya for all 12 months of the tax year
- Maya provided 90% of Noah's support
- Noah provided only 5% of his own support
- Noah is a U.S. citizen
- Noah filed a joint return with his spouse only to claim a refund of withheld tax

Give a short answer first.

## Recorded LLM Answer

Yes. Based on the family relationship, full-year residence, age, and support facts, Noah would be treated as Maya's dependent, and the joint return does not change that here because it was filed only to claim a refund.
